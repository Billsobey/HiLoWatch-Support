Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I restart the game?
A: When the "Game Over" screen appears, simply tap the "Play Again" button. It will appear instantly!
Q: My high score didn't save?
A: High scores are saved automatically to your Apple Watch. If you uninstall the app, your local high score will be reset.
Q: Why don't I see numbers 11, 12, 13?
A: We use standard playing card ranks: Jack (11), Queen (12), King (13), and Ace is Low (1).
