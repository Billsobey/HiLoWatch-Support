## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How do I restart the game?**
A: When the "Game Over" screen appears, simply tap the "Play Again" button. It will appear instantly!

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: **Yes.** HiLoWatch runs 100% on your Apple Watch. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or usage statistics.

**Q: Why don't I see the numbers 1-13?**
A: We use standard playing card ranks:
*   **A (Ace)** = 1 (Low)
*   **J (Jack)** = 11
*   **Q (Queen)** = 12
*   **K (King)** = 13 (High)
